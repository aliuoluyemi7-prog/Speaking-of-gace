A Working Philosophy

Build for the phone in someone's pocket

Every project here is single-file or minimal-file, offline-first, and built without frameworks — because the schools and families they're built for don't have the luxury of a stable connection or a laptop. Software that respects that constraint is software people can actually keep using.
