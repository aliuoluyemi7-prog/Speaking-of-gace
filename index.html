What This Is

Four callings, one conviction

Grace is not a subject to master — it is a lens for every other subject. This work sits at the crossing point of four pursuits.

I

Christian Writer

Theology, exegesis, and reflection written for readers who want depth without abandoning devotion.

II

Teacher

Twenty years in the classroom, shaping how Economics, Government, and Social Studies are taught and understood.

III

AI Builder

Practical, offline-first tools built to serve real people with real constraints — not demos, but working software.

IV

EdTech Creator

School management systems, exam preparation platforms, and digital tools designed for the African classroom.
